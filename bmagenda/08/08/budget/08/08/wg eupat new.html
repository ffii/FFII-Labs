The idea is to "restart" a common group for all patent-related activities and concentrate the know-how of people like Hartmut, Jozef, Benjamin etc, forming a real workgroup with a voting workgroup board that regularly meets and decides. The focus of this wg will be on educational issues (organising a conference or producing an educational brochure is quite self-explaining even to the tax office). Contact with politicians (e.g. when travelling to Bxl for conference preparation) will be a positive side effect. It may also engage in information gathering and provide quality-control for any other patent-related activities that want to enter enter.
