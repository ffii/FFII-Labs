Compromise of some donations and financial data

Dear member of the FFII,

A few days ago, it came to our attention that information for internal
use, such as specific information about donations and other financial
figures, became available via our website. A number of those items
contained information of private nature (we're currently focusing at
about 20 of them); once we learned of this, we immediately remedied the
problem.

The board is now investigating what may need to be changed (through
technical means or otherwise) in relation to treatment of financial
data, and will if appropriate publish updates about this.

Please contact the financial team ( financ-help@ffii.org ) if you are
concerned that you may have been affected and we have not yet contacted
you personally. Alternatively, you may choose to contact the board at:
board@ffii.org

We offer you our most sincere apologies.

On behalf of the board,

--
David Vuorio
Secretary of the FFII

See also explanations of the treasurer.
