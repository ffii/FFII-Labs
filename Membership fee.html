Membership fee

Members help us to have regular financial support to plan with and stay independent. To become a member please register via https://members.ffii.org/register/. The membership fee is currently 36 EUR per year.

Payment

Please refer to the donations page for possible payment methods, but make sure to denote your payment with your FFII user ID and for which year(s) you are paying. If you do not know your FFII user id, please contact our office.

Direct debit

If you have a bank account in Germany you can set up direct debit (Lastschrift) in our membership management system. For membership contributions, this is the most convenient method both for you and for the FFII.

Contact

The office will answer all your questions regarding your membership and participation in FFII activities as a member. Email: office@ffii.org.
